Welcome to the Tigers Taewkwondo Club

Step into the Dojang

Taekwondo, transalating literally into art of kicking and punching, is a martial art hailing from Korea. It was developed during the 1940s and 50s and is based on the earlier form of Korean self-defense known as tae kyon and on karate. Known worldwide for its spectacular kicks and tournaments it is a sport growing in popularity and for good reason.

We here at Tigers have been practicing Taekwondo here in Waterford since 2004 and are proud of our school of 100+ students. We've competed in every tier of competition bringing golds anywhere from regional all the way to international competitions. We honor the Five Tenets of Taekwondo (courtesy, integrity, perseverance, self-control and indomitable spirit) as well as the hidden sixth one, to remember to enjoy yourself!
