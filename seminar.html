Seminar

We Have Once Again Got the Opportunity to Train with the Famous Grand Master Nicholls

For the seventh time in our organisations history we have a chance to train with the world renowned Grand Master Nicholls. We do no take this opportunity lightly and encourage all students who can to participate. It is a rare opportunity to be able to practice with a man anywhere near such a level of experience. The Seminar will take place on the 10th of January.
